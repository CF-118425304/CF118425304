UCC Rugby



This year i joined UCC Rugby and play for the UCC Bulldogs

I had not played rugby in years and decided to join as a form of exercise but

also as an opportunity to meet new people and travel to other colleges.

I have played in many games throughout the year including travelling to Dublin and NUIG.

We train twice a week on a Tuesday and Wednesday night at 18:45.

I was delighted to join a club and be given many opportunities arising from this decision.
